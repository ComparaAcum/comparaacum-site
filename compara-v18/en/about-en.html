About us

Why ComparaAcum.ro exists

Because every Romanian deserves to know they're getting the best price — without spending hours searching for offers

The problem we solve

How many of us have paid the same energy provider, the same car insurance or the same internet subscription for years — not because it was the best, but because we never had time to compare?

The Romanian insurance, energy and telecom market has dozens of providers with hundreds of different offers. Finding and comparing them yourself means hours of searching, phone calls, hard-to-navigate websites and hidden prices buried in small-print contracts.

ComparaAcum.ro was built because Romanians needed a simple, fast and transparent tool — one place where you can see all available offers and choose what suits you best.

What we do differently

⚡
Compare in seconds

Enter your details once and see all available offers — sorted by price, benefits or ratings.

🔍
Full transparency

We display all market offers, not just the ones that pay us more. The price you see is the real price.

🛡️
100% free and independent

We don't sell personal data. We don't charge users. We earn a commission from providers only when someone chooses an offer through our platform.

📋
Everything you need, in one place

Car insurance, home insurance, health insurance, electricity, natural gas, internet and TV — all the important categories on a single platform.

Meet Bruno

Bruno is our bear in a suit — the ComparaAcum.ro mascot and your trusted guide. He’s on your side: he compares dozens of providers for you and shows you exactly where you can save, with no hassle and no hidden costs.

His story: a Transylvanian bear with a knack for numbers who got tired of seeing people overpay just because they were short on time. So he became the guide who compares everything for you.

🐻 “Compare smart, save easy.” — Bruno

Our mission

We want every Romanian to make informed financial decisions — not under the pressure of a sales agent, not out of inertia, not because they didn't have time to find something better.

Romanian consumers are just as smart and demanding as anyone else in Europe. They just needed the right tool. ComparaAcum.ro is that tool.
