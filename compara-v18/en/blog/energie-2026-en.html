Best electricity providers in Romania 2026

The energy market is fully open — here's how to find the cheapest deal for your home

The liberalised energy market

Since the full liberalisation of Romania's energy market, residential customers can freely choose their electricity and gas supplier. This means you are no longer tied to the historical supplier in your area — you can switch to any licensed supplier offering better prices.

Many Romanians are still on default tariffs, which are often not the most competitive. Switching can save 10-25% on your annual energy bill with no interruption to your supply.

What to look for when comparing

💡
Fixed vs. variable tariffs

Fixed tariffs give price certainty for the contract duration. Variable tariffs may be cheaper initially but can rise.

📅
Contract length

Longer contracts (12-24 months) often carry better rates but check cancellation terms.

🌱
Green energy options

Several providers offer 100% renewable electricity at competitive prices.

⭐
Customer service quality

Price isn't everything — check reviews for billing accuracy and complaint handling.
