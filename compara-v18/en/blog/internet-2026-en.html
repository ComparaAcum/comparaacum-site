Which internet provider offers the best value for money in Romania right now?

Romania's internet landscape

Romania has some of the fastest and most affordable broadband in Europe, thanks to strong competition between providers. Fibre-to-the-home (FTTH) coverage is excellent in cities, with speeds from 100Mbps to 1Gbps widely available at competitive prices.

Tips for choosing

  • Check availability at your address first — not all providers cover all areas.
  • Look for promotional prices carefully — introductory rates often jump after 6-12 months.
  • Consider bundling — Internet + TV packages are often cheaper than separate subscriptions.
  • Read the cancellation terms — contracts typically run 12-24 months with early exit fees.
