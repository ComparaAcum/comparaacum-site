Cookie Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This page explains which cookies and similar technologies ComparaAcum.ro uses, why, and how you can manage your preferences.

1. What cookies are

Cookies are small text files stored by your browser on your device. We also use similar technologies such as localStorage, which stores data locally in your browser without automatically sending it to servers.

2. What we use and why

CategoryExamplesPurposeDuration
Strictly necessary (no consent required) ca_consent (localStorage), Supabase session (localStorage), Bruno chat daily limit (localStorage) Remembering your cookie choice, account login, chat functionality Stored locally until deleted / logout
Analytics (only with consent) _ga, _ga_* (Google Analytics 4) Anonymous traffic statistics: pages visited, device type, visit source Up to 24 months

Google Analytics loads only after you click "Accept all" in the cookie banner. If you choose "Essential only", no analytics cookies are set. IP addresses are anonymised.

3. Changing your choice

You can change your option at any time: Change your cookie preferences (the banner will reappear).

You can also delete cookies from your browser settings (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge — Privacy section). Note that clearing local data will log you out of your account.

4. Third-party cookies

When you are redirected to a provider's website, that provider applies its own cookie policy, independent of ours.

5. Contact

Questions about cookies or data protection? Reach us via the Contact page. See also our Privacy policy.
